About ACM

ACM brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. As the world’s largest computing society, ACM strengthens the profession's collective voice through strong leadership, promotion of the highest standards, and recognition of technical excellence. ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life‐long learning, career development, and professional networking.

How to Join

What We get as a Student Chapter

  • Administrative Interface
  • Chapter Web Tools
  • Local Activities Calendar
  • Chapter Member Locator
  • ACM Distinguished Speakers Program
  • UPE/ACM Student Chapter Scholarship Award
  • ACM Student Chapter Excellence Program
  • Promotional Materials

More information at: https://www.acm.org/chapters/students

What we need to have a chapter

  1. We need 10 paying members.
  2. We need a Chair, Vice Chair, and Treasurer. All of which must be members.
  3. We need a faculty member.
  4. We need chapter name, chapter email address, chapter type, and chapter sub-type.

    5. More information at: https://www.acm.org/chapters/students/how-to-start-a-student-chapter
